Capabilities

Built for precision and privacy

Every photo is processed, embedded, and encrypted. Originals never leave your device permanently.

🧠
Semantic embeddings
Sentence Transformers (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) generates 384-d vectors that understand meaning — "vehicle" matches cars, bikes, and trucks.
🤖
AI captions (BLIP)
BLIP automatically describes every photo — "a red car parked on a street at night" — making search language-aware.
📝
OCR text extraction
Tesseract reads text inside your photos — receipts, menus, signs, documents — making them fully searchable.
🔐
AES-256 encrypted
Every embedding is encrypted at rest with Fernet AES-256. Your originals are deleted immediately after processing.
How it works

Four steps to searchable memories

From upload to searchable in seconds.

01
Upload
Drop photos into the app. JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF — up to 50MB each.
02
AI processing
BLIP generates captions, Tesseract extracts text, Sentence Transformers builds a 384-d embedding vector.
03
Encrypt & store
Raw pixels discarded. Only the AES-256 encrypted embedding and metadata are saved.
04
Search
Type anything. Cosine similarity finds the most semantically relevant photos in milliseconds.
